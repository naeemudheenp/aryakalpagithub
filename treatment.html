Shirodhara

The name comes from the sanskrit words 'Shiro'(head) and 'dhara'(flow).It is an Ayurvedic therapy, gently pouring of liquids over the forehead the method is included in Panchakarma.
Helps to reduce stress,reduce anxiety,improve sleep quality.Very effective in cervical spondylosis.


Netradhara

Pouring of medicated ghee into the eyes ,where it is kept for a specific time that improves eye sight and it is recommended for strained eyes and used for cooling and relaxation of eyes.


Uzhichil(Abhyangam)

Body uzhichil is a very importatnt massage therapy which reduces the damage and illness of the body .Herbal oil is used for this therapy which is done by expert masseur.Impacts to all body parts and sense organs. Improves muscle tone,Tones up blood vessels,Nerves.Helps to cure low back ache,muscle pain,joint pain,lumbar spondylitis,cervical spondylitis.



Patrapinda sweda

Rejuvunates and strengthens soft tissues joints and muscles.Indcated in osteoarthritis,sciatica-
-spondylitis,spondylosis,sprains,cramps,sports injuries.



Podi kizhi

The medicated powder made into a bolus and tied by using a cloth which is then heated and applied over the body or affected area.Imparts blood circulation,alleviates pain and reduces swelling.Indicated in sciatica,athritis ,rheumatoid arthritis,muscular spasm,osteo arthritis.



Nasyam

Administarting of the medical drugs through the route of nasal cavity which cleanses ,purifies and strengthens nasal cavity.Benificial for migraine,allergies sinusitis,rhinitis.




Shashtikasali pinda swedam (njavara kizhi)

It is performed on full body or half body with the bolus of shashitikasali(njavara) medicated kashayam and milk.Relives pain and stifiness,improves muscle strength, reenergise and rejuvunites body.Helps to cure muscular pain,rhemuatoid arthritis,osteo arthritis,joint stiffness, pain and disc prolapse.



Janu Vasti

Pouring of warm medicated oil and keeping for a specific time on knee joint.Good for reducing knee joint pain and degenerative condition of knee joint,good for smooth knee joint movement,strengthens and improves circulations.
